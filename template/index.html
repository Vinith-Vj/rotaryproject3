Welcome to Rotary Club

Where every hand extends hope and every heart embraces change.

Welcome to the Rotary Club of Thangasseri, a vibrant community dedicated to service above self. Our club, located in the historic coastal town of Thangasseri, brings together professionals and community leaders committed to making a positive impact locally and globally. Through various projects and initiatives, we aim to address pressing issues such as education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and community development. Join us in our mission to create lasting change and foster goodwill. Together, we can build a better future for all.
